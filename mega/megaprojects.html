Somaiya Aeromodelling Cell (Team Onyx)

Faculty Advisor: Prof. Prashant Jain

Team Onyx is a team of zealous mechanical engineering students from K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering with sound technical knowledge and a creative mind frame. They are dedicated to the hobby of aeromodelling. It was established in year 2011 for the design and manufacture of radio controlled, heavy lifting aircraft model. The team represents the college every year at an international aeromodelling competition named “SAE Aero Design West” in the USA. It’s a platform for students to exhibit their practical knowledge and develop them as confident individuals for a stronger tomorrow.

Orion Racing

Faculty Advisor: Prof. B.M. Pradhan

Orion Racing India was born In 2006, a student initiative which has today become a craze among budding automobile enthusiasts and a force to be reckoned with amongst its competitors. Consistently adjudged the best Asian team at Formula Student Germany, ORI has brought India laurels and won accolades for its performance. In addition to achieving technical brilliance ORI has also won acclaim in team cooperation and resource management, having been placed 3rd at the Cost Report event in FSG’10. Orion Racing has since, come a long way from a hobby idea to a semi-professional project par excellence and in 2012 they changed the Indian Motorsport scenario by accomplishing the tough Endurance race, becoming the only Indian Team to do it.
For more information: Website Facebook

Red Shift Racing

Faculty Advisor: Prof. S.S Bhusnoor

Started in 2009 Red Shift Racing’s key motive is to compete with top teams all over the globe in an SAE organized Baja event. Red Shift Racing has since, come a long way from a hobby idea to a semi-professional project par excellence. Participated in SAE BAJA Auburn event held at USA in the year 2012 and SAE BAJA Indore Event held at INDORE in 2013.

Robocon

Faculty Advisor: Prof. Avinash Prabhu Desai

KJSCE Robocon is a team dedicated to the field of robotics. Founded in 2010 by group of students, Team KJSCE Robocon has paved the way for young intelligent minds to practice and implement new and innovative designs in modern day robotics. With the sole agenda of winning Robocon India - National level robotics competition , they intend representing India at the international level. With a national rank of 10 in the year 2012, the strength and appeal of Team KJSCE Robocon is reaching new heights. Team KJSCE Robocon is a group dedicated to fields of robotics. We have been representing K.J.Somaiya College of Engineering,Vidyavihar at Robocon since 2011. The team comprises of students from mechanical, electronics, extc and computer department.
